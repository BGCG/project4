Plant-based recipes for all.

Here at VeganVivera we provide a platform for users to share plant-based recipes. Switching partially or completely to a plant-based diet can be overwhelming as many of our favourite dishes often require animal products. Here we and the commnuity provide recipes of varying difficulties - allowing users of varying confidence levels in cooking to try plant-based recipes. Whether your concerned about animal welfare, the environment, or simply just want to focus on eating healthier - this is the place for you! If you would like to post your own plant-based recipes, interact with the conversation or create your own favourite recipe list - please sign up with us and join the community!
